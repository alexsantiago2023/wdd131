Final Project Site Plan

Site Name:

A good name would be Rexburg Snowboarding.This name shows that the website is about snowboarding for people who live close to Rexburg. A good domain could be rexburg-snow.org or something similar.

Site Purpose:

This website will provide information on the ski resorts close to Rexburg along with links their own websites.

Scenarios

What are the pros and cons of the different ski resorts around my area?
What are the prices of each ski resort?
How can I get in contact with their customer service?

Color Schema

I will use white, dark blue and light blue. Light blue will be the background color for the header and footer and dark blue will be the text color of the headers and the navigation background.

Typography

header font: Averia Serif Libre

paragraph font: Roboto
